About Psephia

Psephia is a new social media website by RobTechUK, inspired by the Ancient Greek word psephia, meaning pebble, which was once used by people to cast their vote. Because of this practice, the word psephos evolved to mean a vote or a decree. The terms psephology is the scientific study of elections.

The site allows users to submit questions for other users to vote on, or to vote on topics submitted by others.

A post can remain open for up to 7 days, and once voting has closed, the result will be revealed. Users will not be able to see how others have voted until the voting period has ended.

Once a vote has ended, comments will be allowed on the post so users can discuss the result.

General Rules

We want to keep debate on Psephia civil, respectful, and lawful. RobTechUK may remove posts or comments that incite violence, promote hatred, harass other users, encourage illegal activity, or otherwise violate UK law.

Repeated breaches of these rules may result in content removal or restricted access to the site. For further information, please see our Community Guidelines.
